2020 was a year of growth and introspection for many people, myself included. I had an urgency to push myself, to learn, to broaden my horizons. I had the drive, but lacked an outlet for that energy until I enrolled in a Web Development program through Eleven Fifty Academy. During my time at this immersive six month boot camp, I was taught the foundations of JavaScript, TypeScript, React, RESTful APIs, SQL server, CSS, SCSS, and HTML5. There is still much to learn and so much more to create, and that is what excites me the most!
